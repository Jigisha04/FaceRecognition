How to Use

Step 1: Real-Time Detection

Click on the “Try Now” button to activate the food detection feature. Use your device camera to scan your food, and let our AI analyze it in real time.

Step 2: Allow camera access

Click on the Start Detection button and allow camera access to get detailed insights into the food items identified.

Step 3: View Nutritional Analysis

Access a comprehensive breakdown of the nutrients in your meals to align with your health goals.

Step 4: Explore Recipes

Based on your detected food items, explore our curated list of personalized recipes to make the most of your ingredients.

Step 5: Plan Your Meals

Use our meal planning feature to organize balanced meals for the day or week, tailored to your dietary preferences.
